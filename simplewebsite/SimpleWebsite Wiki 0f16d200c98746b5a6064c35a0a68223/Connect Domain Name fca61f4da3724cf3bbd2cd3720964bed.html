01. Purchase a Domain Name

How to Purchase a Domain Name

  1. Choose a domain registrar: There are many domain registrars available online such as GoDaddy, Namecheap, and Google Domains. Choose one that suits your needs.
  1. Search for available domain names: Enter your desired domain name in the registrar's search bar to check its availability. If the name is already taken, the registrar will suggest variations or alternatives.
  1. Choose your domain name: Once you find an available domain name that you like, add it to your cart and proceed to checkout.
  1. Enter your information: You will need to provide your name, address, and payment information to complete the purchase.
  1. Complete the purchase: Review your order and make the payment to complete the purchase.

Free Domain Name Services

  • Freenom: This service provides free domain names with extensions such as .tk, .ml, .ga, .cf, and .gq.
  • Dot.tk: This service provides free domain names with the .tk extension.

Remember that free domain name services may come with certain limitations or restrictions. It's important to read the terms and conditions carefully before using them.

02. Configure DNS

Add following records to your domain’s dns as following example.

  1. A Record: 122.122.33.4 (Your Server IP)
  1. SRV Record :
    • Service: _http
    • Protocol: _tcp
    • Priority: 0
    • Weight: 5
    • Port: 8048 (Your Website Port)
    • Target: yourdomain.com (Your Domain Name).
